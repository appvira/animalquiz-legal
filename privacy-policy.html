Privacy Policy

Animal Quiz! by APPVIRA LLC
Last updated: April 6, 2025

Animal Quiz! is developed by APPVIRA LLC ("we", "our", or "us"). Our app is designed for young children and we take your family's privacy seriously. We are committed to complying with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and applicable privacy laws.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information from children. The app does not require an account, login, or any personal details to play.

In-App Purchases

Animal Quiz! offers an optional one-time "Animal Match Pro" unlock through the Apple App Store and Google Play Store. Purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple and Google. We use RevenueCat (revenuecat.com) to verify your purchase entitlement. RevenueCat may receive an anonymous device identifier and purchase receipt to verify your one-time purchase. No name, email address, or other personal information is shared with RevenueCat.

Audio & Text-to-Speech

Animal sounds and text-to-speech (animal names spoken aloud) are processed entirely on-device. No audio is recorded, transmitted, or stored.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat — purchase verification (no personal data from children)
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — payment processing
  • Google Fonts — font delivery (standard CDN request, no personal data)

We do not use advertising networks, analytics SDKs, or social media integrations.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any user data with third parties beyond what is described above for purchase verification.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Animal Quiz! is directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. A parental gate is shown before any purchase flow to ensure a parent or guardian is present for all transactions.

Data Retention

Because we do not collect personal data, there is nothing to retain or delete. Purchase records are managed by your App Store account and are handled by Apple and Google per their respective store policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with a new "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
